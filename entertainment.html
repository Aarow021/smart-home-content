Entertainment

This smart house features many systems to keep your day enjoyable!

Nursery

Have children? Or just need a place to relax? Our nursery can accommodate both needs! With a stunning crystal display of animated nature, you can peacefully gaze at giraffes, panthers, butterflies, and many more from the comfort of your own home!

Automatic Table

Lunch does not need to be a bore, when our special tables come to the fore! At any time of your choosing, our automated patio table will pop out fully prepared for a lunch full of fun! Supports cards, drinks, food, music and more!

Fireplace

If you ever need to relax, our fireplace is perfect for taking a nap or reading a book.

Poetry reader

Want a bedtime story? Our built-in poetry reader will fulfill your needs! Choose the poem you want to read, or let it select one for you.
